Short Bio

I am a Professor leading the Plant Nutrition and Fertilization Technology Lab at Institute of Agricultural Environment and Resource, Shanxi Academy of Agricultural Sciences. I spent one year at University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa with Russell S. Yost as a visiting scholar. Prior to my visit at University of Hawaiʻi, I spent 6 months at University of Melbourne with Deli Chen as a visiting scholar

I obtained my Ph.D. at Shanxi University in Ecology under the supervision of MingChang Chen. I completed my undergraduate study at Shanxi Agricultural University in Soil Chemistry.

My research interest includes crop integrated nutrient management, saline-alkali improvement, and fertilizer R&D
